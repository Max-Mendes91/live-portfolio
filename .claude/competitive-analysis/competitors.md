# Competitive Analysis - Web Development Market

> **Analysis Date:** January 2026
> **Target Markets:** Częstochowa, Śląsk, Poland, UK, US, EU
> **Focus:** Local agencies, SaaS development, international freelancers

---

## Executive Summary

### Market Gaps Identified
1. **No SaaS focus in Częstochowa** - All competitors focus on WordPress/CMS websites
2. **No international targeting** - All local competitors target Polish market only
3. **Outdated tech stacks** - WordPress, Joomla, Drupal dominate; no React/Next.js
4. **No startup/MVP services** - Only Saasco (Wrocław) offers this
5. **Weak English presence** - No bilingual sites targeting UK/US clients

### Your Competitive Advantage
- Modern stack: React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js
- SaaS/MVP development capability
- Bilingual (EN/PL) targeting international clients
- UK/US timezone-friendly
- Full-stack (not just frontend WordPress)
